Section 3 Practical Tasks

Practice configuring general call center settings.

In the sandbox environment, apply the General Settings configuration covered in the video. Focus on understanding each setting's impact on day-to-day call center operations.
  • ✓
    Configure the tenant name, contact information, and default timezone in General Settings.
  • ✓
    Set up at least one Business Hours schedule and assign it to a service.
  • ✓
    Enable and configure call recording settings — understand the difference between on-demand and always-on recording.
  • ✓
    Locate the Voicemail and Callback settings and describe what each controls.
  • ✓
    Review the Security settings tab — identify at least 3 options and explain their purpose.

Section 4 Practical Tasks

Create and manage users, teams, and services in the sandbox.

Build out a working organizational structure in the sandbox by creating users, assigning them to teams, configuring privilege roles, and defining a service end-to-end.
  • ✓
    Create 3 user accounts with different roles: Agent, Supervisor, and Administrator. Note the key differences in access level for each.
  • ✓
    Create a Team, assign all 3 users to it, and set the Supervisor as the team lead.
  • ✓
    Review the default Privilege Sets — identify which privileges are granted to each role by default and customize one.
  • ✓
    Create a new Voice Service called "Inbound Support", configure its queue settings, and assign the team to it.
  • ✓
    Explain in your own words: what is the difference between a Service and a Scenario in Bright Pattern?
